About lessons

Lessons are designed to be clear, encouraging, and practical — whether you’re building fundamentals, preparing for band/orchestra, or working toward auditions and performances.

In-person & online

Choose the format that fits your life. Online lessons can work great with the right setup, and I’ll help you keep it simple.

What students commonly focus on

Snare drum and mallets, reading and rhythm, technique and sound quality, ensemble skills, and performance confidence.

How scheduling works

Current clients book lessons through a private scheduling page. If you’re interested in starting lessons, reach out first and I’ll share next steps.
