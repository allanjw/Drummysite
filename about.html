About

Percussion lessons are clear, encouraging, and practical — designed to help students build strong fundamentals, grow confidence, and make steady musical progress.

I work with beginners who are just getting started, students preparing for school ensembles, and advancing players refining technique and musicianship. Lessons are structured around each student’s goals, with an emphasis on consistency, listening, and musical understanding.

In-person & online lessons

Lessons are available in person or online. With a simple setup, online lessons can be just as effective, and I’ll help you choose an approach that works well for your space and schedule.

Common areas of focus

Snare drum and mallet instruments, reading and rhythm, technique and sound quality, ensemble skills, audition preparation, and performance confidence.

How scheduling works

Current clients schedule lessons through a private online booking system. If you’re interested in starting lessons, please reach out through the contact page and I’ll share availability and next steps.

What to expect

Lessons are supportive and focused, with clear expectations and achievable goals. We’ll track progress over time and adjust plans as skills and interests develop.
